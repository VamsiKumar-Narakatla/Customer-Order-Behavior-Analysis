## ğŸ” Business Questions Answered

The analysis focuses on **practical business questions**:

1. What are the overall business KPIs (total customers, total orders, total revenue)?
2. How frequently do customers place orders?
3. How many customers are repeat buyers vs one-time buyers?
4. What is the average order value?
5. Who are the top revenue-generating customers?
6. How does revenue change month over month?
7. What is the lifetime value of each customer?
8. How long do customers remain active?
9. Which customers are inactive recently?
10. Which product categories contribute the most revenue?
